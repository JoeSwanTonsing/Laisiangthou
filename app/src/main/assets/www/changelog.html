Hiai a nuai a te Laisiangthou 1.0 apat 3.0 tan a a-feature kikhek tuamtuam te athei ut te adia chiamtehna ahi.
    Latest Version 3.0:
    Version 3.0 a kikhek te:
  • 3.0 a hong kichih naziak ahihleh a layout kikheng lamdang zek a-om nawn ziak ahi.
  • ViewPager zang a tabs nih a Thukhunlui leh Thukhunthak a kibawl pen kilakhia in, Laibu tengteng ki list suk in, Thukhunlui leh Thukhunthak rong tuam a ki mark, banah list leh grid khengtuah theih dia kibawl daih. Hiai khah performance optimize na dia kikhek hideuh ahi.
  • Theme khek na ding homepage a drawer pen a dialog dan a kikoih, baihlam tak a zattheih dia.
  • Online Bible Commentaries Bible pumpi a ding baihlam tak a access theih dia kikoih. Hiai zattheih na dia internet ngai ding. A link-te khatvei va click inla a webpage load leteh, Laisiangthou cache na clear kei nakleh ana ki download/save ding. Huaikhah, na load thak non chiah internet lou ale paithei ta ding.
    Hiai commentaries te khah Laisiangthou pumpi ading ahi a, Sappau ahi a Easy English te website apat kilasawn ahi.
  • Rate button leh feedback piakna Floating Button a kibawl pen kilakhia in, ActionBar/Toolbar a kikoihtouh.
